## üõ†Ô∏è Installation & Setup

Getting started is simple:

1.  **Install the Extension**: Visit the [Chrome Web Store](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/youtube-studio-assistant/gbhimfdbedcgofieikihfacihgblpcpg) and click "Add to Chrome".
2.  **Connect Your AI**: Open the extension's settings in YouTube Studio and add your API key for Gemini, OpenAI, or DeepSeek.
3.  **Start Creating**: The assistant panel will now be available on your video detail pages.

## üîí Privacy & Security

Your privacy is our top priority.

- **Local Storage**: All your API keys and settings are stored securely on your local machine. They are never sent to our servers.
- **Direct API Calls**: The extension communicates directly with the AI providers you choose. We do not proxy or log your requests.
- **Anonymous Analytics**: We collect minimal, non-identifiable usage data to improve the extension. You can opt-out at any time in the settings.

For full details, please read our [Privacy Policy](https://youtubestudioassistant.website/privacy-policy).
